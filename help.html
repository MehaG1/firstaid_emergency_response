The Patient Is:

Unconcious

Unresponsive/Not Breathing

Step One: Have Someone Call 911
Step Two: Perform CPR (30 compressions, 5 cycles, switch)
Step Three: Have someone look for and use an AED

Choking

Difficulty Breathing

Step One: Ask Permission to Step In
Step Two: wrap your hands around the top of their waist, one hand in a fist and the other covering it.
Step Three: Push in and up.

Burned

With a Chemical

Step One: Call 911 for large burns, check for safety, and flush the affected area.
Step Two: Remove clothes/jewelry in contact with the chemical
Step Three: Bandage the area.

Burned

By Heat/Fire

Step One: Call 911 for serious burns; Cool the burn with cool water (no ice, butter, etc.)
Step Two: Remove nearby, not stuck, clothing and keep patient warm.
Step Three: Cover with a thin film (eg plastic) and take painkillers.
