About InstaMed

We are a team looking to create a safer, more efficient world through the implementation of a system that allows first aid knowledge to be learned, shared, and put into practice wherever it is needed. We hope to develop a system where our users can build their own first aid skills to help others, and those who are already knowledgable can put their experience to use in real-life situations where they can make a measurable impact in keeping others safe.

Especially throughout the pandemic of the last year, we've witnessed firsthand the ways that technology can be utilized to teach, communicate, and assist, and we've created a platform where those tools can be taken advantage of. Whether you're here for assistance, to assist, or to learn, know that our goal is to improve the safety of our community in every way possible.
